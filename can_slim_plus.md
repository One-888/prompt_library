Instead of treating all timeframes equally, the model assigns **greater weight to more recent performance**. - **Immediate momentum** (useful for breakout timing)

This weighted approach allows the system to:

- Prioritize **stocks accelerating now**, not just those with historical strength
- Identify **emerging leaders** before they appear on traditional long-term RS screens
- Maintain **flexibility** to adjust lookback periods or weights as market conditions evolve
